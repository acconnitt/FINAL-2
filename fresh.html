ABOUT OUR BUSINESS

Founded in 2000, we are the pioneers in the 100% organic fruits in the California area. We have been serving in Carlsbad for the past 22 years and we currently have 3 locations – on Los Angeles, San Diego. We offer catering for any special events! Bring your friends and Family!

100% fresh products & delicious food. We offer a variety of healthy, wholesome and nutritious fresh products essential to a healthy lifestyle. Whether you are looking for a healthy lunchtime snack, or want to give someone a tasty special treat, we have the right food for you.

Friendly staff & relaxing atmosphere. Everyone who works here is friendly, fun and easy to talk to. In this way we make you feel relaxed. Our place is relaxing and comfortable so you can enjoy your food and still feel comfortable.
